About MyWeb

MyWeb is a platform built by students, for students. Our mission is to empower young minds to learn, share, and connect in a safe and inspiring environment.

Our Journey

  • 2022: MyWeb idea was born during a school hackathon.
  • 2023: First version launched with basic project sharing.
  • 2024: Community grew to 500+ users and new features were added.
  • 2025: Introduced blog posts, calculators, and more!
